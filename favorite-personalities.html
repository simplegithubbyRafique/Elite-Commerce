Imran Khan

Imran Khan is a prominent figure in Pakistani politics and beyond. Born on October 5, 1952, in Lahore, Pakistan, Khan initially gained fame as a world-class cricketer, leading Pakistan to victory in the 1992 Cricket World Cup. After retiring from cricket, he transitioned into politics, founding the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) party in 1996 with a vision of creating a "Naya Pakistan" (New Pakistan) based on justice, equality, and anti-corruption. Imran Khan's political journey saw its culmination when he was sworn in as the 22nd Prime Minister of Pakistan on August 18, 2018.

Maulana Tariq Jameel emphasizes the importance of self-reflection, compassion, and adherence to Islamic principles in leading a meaningful life. His speeches and sermons have inspired millions to strive for spiritual growth and contribute positively to society.
