My Favorite Tourist Spots

Trail 5 Islamabad

Trail 5 in Islamabad is one of the most popular hiking trails in the capital city of Pakistan. Located in the scenic Margalla Hills National Park, Trail 5 offers breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and Islamabad cityscape. It is a favorite destination for both locals and tourists seeking outdoor adventures and a break from the hustle and bustle of urban life. The trail begins near the Faisal Mosque and winds its way through the lush greenery of the Margalla Hills. Along the way, hikers encounter diverse flora and fauna, including various species of birds and small mammals. The trail is well-maintained and relatively easy to navigate, making it suitable for hikers of all skill levels. At the end of the trail, hikers are rewarded with stunning panoramic views of Islamabad and the picturesque landscape below. Many visitors choose to trek to the viewpoint at the summit of the trail to capture breathtaking sunset or sunrise scenes. Trail 5 is not just a hiking destination; it also serves as a recreational hub for families and friends looking to spend quality time outdoors. With its serene ambiance, natural beauty, and invigorating hiking experience, Trail 5 continues to be a cherished destination for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts alike.

Ansoo Lake

At an altitude of 16,492 feet (5,032 meters) above sea level, Ansoo Lake is a breathtaking high-altitude lake nestled in the Kaghan Valley of Pakistan's Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province. Its name, "Ansoo," translates to "teardrop" in Urdu, owing to the lake's unique tear-like shape. Surrounded by towering peaks and accessible only via a challenging trek, Ansoo Lake is a hidden gem for adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts. The trek to Ansoo Lake is renowned for its stunning vistas of the Himalayan landscape, alpine meadows, and glacial streams. The journey typically begins from the picturesque village of Saiful Muluk and entails a rigorous ascent through rugged terrain, passing through dense forests and rocky paths. Along the way, trekkers are treated to panoramic views of snow-capped peaks, including Malika Parbat and Makkra Peak. After several hours of trekking, adventurers reach the tranquil shores of Ansoo Lake, where the shimmering waters reflect the surrounding mountains, creating a mesmerizing sight. The lake's remote location and pristine beauty make it a perfect spot for camping, allowing visitors to immerse themselves in the serenity of the wilderness. Ansoo Lake remains a relatively untouched destination, drawing intrepid travelers seeking an off-the-beaten-path experience. While the trek to Ansoo Lake demands physical endurance and proper preparation, the awe-inspiring beauty of this natural wonder makes it a truly unforgettable adventure.
